Danao Technological College



VISION


DTC Envisions to become an International-College in Science and Technology committed to provide quality and excellence in Techno-Tourism and Agri-Tourism for the total.


MISSION


DTC visualize to become the Center of Excellence in Techno-Tourism and Agri-Tourism providing knowledge, skills, and attitudes that would transform the world digitally.


GOAL


  • to implement the programs activities and development of Techno-Tourism, Agri-Tourism and Performing Arts through Information Technology.

  • to promote community awareness and appreciation of Filipino Culture and the environmental conversation through art and tourism.

  • to augment Good Moral and Right Conduct(GMRC) and spirituals upliftment of DTCians community.

  • to establish industry-academe linkages and partnership for On-The-Job Training Program.

  • to organize and strengthen DTC research agenda in according with CHED National Research Agenda.


    • OBJECTIVES


  • to participate in any Local and International Tourism Film Festival and competitions to gain recognition for Danao Technological College(DTC) specially in the cultural and historical revival of Danao, Bohol.

  • to conduct institutional experimental research and publish in any CHED accredited journals.

  • to help the family in the community uplift their standards of living and support sustainable livelihood program.

  • to develop Danao, Bohol into a Techno-Tourism and Agri-Tourism hub in the country and the world.

  • to strengthen school-community outreach program in every barangay of Danao, Bohol.
